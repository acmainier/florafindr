FloraFindr — Brand Description

Brand Essence
FloraFindr is a modern, friendly plant‑discovery brand designed for curious gardeners, hobby botanists, and nature lovers. It blends technology + nature in a way that feels warm, intuitive, and playful. The brand’s personality is approachable, knowledgeable, and quietly stylish — never clinical, never childish.

Brand Story
FloraFindr exists to make plant knowledge accessible. Whether someone is identifying a wildflower on a walk, researching a houseplant, or exploring botanical families, FloraFindr turns curiosity into clarity.
It’s built on the idea that every plant has a story, and discovering it should feel delightful.

Brand Values
Discovery — Encourages exploration and learning
Clarity — Makes plant information simple and visual
Warmth — Friendly tone, gentle colours, soft shapes
Trust — Accurate data, reliable search, clean design

Visual Identity
Colour Direction
A soft, modern palette inspired by nature and digital minimalism:
Pastel Green (#CDE8C5) — freshness, growth
Midnight Blue (#1A2A40) — depth, trust, contrast
Blush Pink (#F2D7D9) — warmth, friendliness
Gold Accent (#D9A441) — highlights, calls to action

This palette feels botanical without being cliché.

Logo Concept
A stylised flower with one petal shaped like a search icon.
The shape is simple enough to work as an app icon, favicon, or stamp.

The mark communicates:
🌸 “This is about plants”  
🔍 “This is about finding things”

Typography
Heading: A rounded geometric sans (e.g., Poppins, Nunito)

Body: A clean humanist sans (e.g., Inter, Lato)

This combination keeps the brand modern, readable, and friendly.

Tone of Voice
FloraFindr speaks like a knowledgeable friend:

Warm, not overly technical

Encouraging, curious, and clear

Uses simple language with gentle personality

Avoids jargon unless the user wants depth

Example tone:
“Let’s explore this plant together.”
“Here’s what makes this species special.”

Tagline Options:

Find the plants you love.

Discover nature, one leaf at a time.

Search. Learn. Grow.

Your pocket guide to the plant world.


User Experience Style
The UI should feel:

Light, airy, and uncluttered

Rounded edges, soft shadows

Plenty of white space

Plant images showcased prominently

Search bar as the hero element

Think: calm, modern, botanical tech.
