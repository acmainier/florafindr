About FloraFindr

FloraFindr is a modern, friendly plant‑discovery brand designed for curious gardeners, hobby botanists, and nature lovers. It blends technology + nature in a way that feels warm, intuitive, and playful. The brand’s personality is approachable, knowledgeable, and quietly stylish — never clinical, never childish.

Brand Story

FloraFindr exists to make plant knowledge accessible. Whether someone is identifying a wildflower on a walk, researching a houseplant, or exploring botanical families, FloraFindr turns curiosity into clarity.

It’s built on the idea that every plant has a story, and discovering it should feel delightful.
